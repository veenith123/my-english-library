I LIKE BATS

I Like bats

Hanging upside down

Like rats. Like silk-cotton fruits

Swinging in wind

What a way to relax and rewind.

I wish I could

Do that

Like a bat

A way to find

After a day's work

To relax and rewind.

Upside down

Hang like bats.

To like bats

Hanging upside down

Like rats.
